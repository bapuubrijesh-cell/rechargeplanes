Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: 24 June 2026

1. Successful Recharge

Successful recharge ya bill payment ko cancel nahi kiya ja sakta, kyunki amount turant operator ke paas chala jaata hai.

2. Failed Transactions

Agar paise kat gaye lekin recharge fail ho gaya, to amount automatically 3–7 working days me aapke original payment method me refund ho jaata hai.

3. Wrong Number Recharge

Agar aapne galat number par recharge kar diya, to Rechup Nex usme refund nahi de sakta, kyunki amount us number par credit ho chuka hota hai.

4. How to Request a Refund

  • Apni transaction ID ke saath humein contact karein.
  • Hum 24–48 ghante me aapke request ki jaanch karenge.
  • Eligible refund 3–7 working days me process kiya jayega.

5. Contact

Refund se related help ke liye: support@rechupnex.com (apna asli email yahan daalein).
