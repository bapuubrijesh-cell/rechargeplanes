Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 June 2026

Rechup Nex ("we", "us", "our") aapki privacy ka samman karta hai. Yeh policy batati hai ki hum aapki information kaise collect, use aur protect karte hain jab aap hamari website ka use karte hain.

1. Information We Collect

  • Mobile number, operator aur recharge amount jo aap recharge ke liye enter karte hain.
  • Payment information (payment hamare secure payment partners ke through process hota hai; hum aapke full card details store nahi karte).
  • Basic technical data jaise device type, browser aur IP address.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Aapke recharge aur bill payment requests ko process karne ke liye.
  • Customer support aur transaction updates dene ke liye.
  • Service ko improve aur secure rakhne ke liye.

3. Data Sharing

Hum aapki personal information bechte nahi hain. Information sirf telecom operators aur payment processors ke saath share ki jaati hai jitni recharge complete karne ke liye zaroori ho, ya jab kanoon ke tahat zaroori ho.

4. Data Security

Hum industry-standard security measures (encryption, secure payment gateways) ka use karte hain taaki aapki information surakshit rahe.

5. Cookies

Website behtar experience dene ke liye cookies ka use kar sakti hai. Aap apne browser se cookies disable kar sakte hain.

6. Your Rights

Aap apni personal data ko access, correct ya delete karwane ke liye humse contact kar sakte hain.

7. Contact Us

Is policy ke baare me koi sawaal ho to humein contact karein: support@rechupnex.com (apna asli email yahan daalein).
